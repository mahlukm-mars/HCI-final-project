About Strategic 8

Strategic 8 Tax Consulting Firm is headquartered at World Trade Centre (WTC), Jl. Jend. Sudirman Kav. 29-31, Jakarta, with branch offices in Bekasi and Manado.

Since 2007, we have been officially licensed by the Director General of Taxes as a Registered Tax Consultant. In 2010, we established our firm under the name Vaudy Starworld and Partners Tax Consulting Firm. As tax consultants, we provide comprehensive services in taxation including Tax Disputes, Tax Facilities, Tax Planning, Affiliate Transactions, Tax Compliance Research, Current Year Tax Compliance, Tax Assistance, Tax Training, Payroll and Employee Tax Services, and Recruitment Services.

Since 2007, we have represented taxpayers as Tax Attorneys in Tax Court, handling disputes related to both central and regional taxes, as well as customs and excise matters. Since 2010, we have been certified as Registered Customs Experts and are members of the Indonesian Customs Expert Association (PERAKI), specializing in customs dispute litigation and providing support during audits and objections.
